# Command: Signal Advisor

Run this advisor when the session produces a decision, lesson, or pattern that
should survive beyond the current chat.

## Watch For

- choices that will shape future work
- newly confirmed constraints or preferences
- fixes, failures, or workflows likely to repeat
- observations that should become teaching, state, or routing signal

## Behavior

1. Identify the candidate signal.
2. Explain why it is likely to matter again.
3. Recommend the most appropriate durable home.
4. Avoid noise by ignoring one-off details with little reuse value.
